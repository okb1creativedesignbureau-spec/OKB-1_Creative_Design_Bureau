- Vrms, Vpp, phase change, impedance vs frequency
- Raw pre-experiment values + oscilloscope captures
AC Signal Behavior from DPDT Switching with DC Source
- Signal change after switching
- Voltage vector tables & comparison to theory
- Simulation data included
AC Response of Resistors
- Vrms, Vpeak-to-peak, current estimation
- Impedance calculations
- Measured vs theoretical comparison
AC Response of Capacitors
- Impedance (Xc) at different frequencies
- Phase shift behavior
- Voltage vector diagrams & total voltage

AC Response of Inductors

- Proteus simulation screenshots for reference
